# Nortal Company Background Research

*Research date: 2026-03-17*

## 1. Company Overview

- **HQ**: Tallinn, Estonia (founded 2000 as "Webmedia")
- **Ownership**: 100% employee-owned since 2016
- **FY2024 Revenue**: EUR 228.7 million (EBITDA EUR 27.9M)
- **Employees**: 2,000+ across 30 locations
- **Presence**: 15 home markets across 4 continents, solutions deployed in 60 countries

## 2. Core Service Lines

- **Strategy and Transformation** — business and digital strategy consulting
- **Data and Artificial Intelligence** — ML, generative AI, edge AI, data platforms
- **Technology and Engineering** — custom software development, platform builds
- **Platform Optimization** — legacy modernization, cloud migration
- **Product and Experience** — UX/UI, product design
- **Cyber Resilience** — cybersecurity, defense (2024 acquisition of UK firm 3DOT Solutions)
- **IT Outsourcing / Staff Augmentation** — nearshore delivery via Latin American talent (Nearsure acquisition)

## 3. AI & Digital Transformation Offerings

- **AI-Driven Legacy Modernization**: Proprietary "AI Legacy Archaeology" — uses AI to analyze millions of lines of legacy code, databases, and documentation, producing structured maps. Accelerates migration projects.
- **Enterprise AI Agents**: Building autonomous AI agents for business function orchestration — beyond copilots to full decision-making systems.
- **Microsoft Fabric / Azure Data Platforms**: Microsoft Fabric Featured Partner, Solutions Partner in Data & AI and Digital & App Innovation, Azure Analytics specialization. Unified analytics platforms, data engineering pipelines, AI-ready data foundations.
- **Industrial AI**: Featured in Microsoft's Industrial AI Partner Guide. Manufacturing and energy clients (predictive maintenance, energy forecasting).
- **GenAI Internal Use**: In-house GenAI agent for hiring processes.

## 4. Geographic Presence

- **Nordics/Baltics**: Estonia (HQ), Finland, Lithuania
- **Central/Eastern Europe**: Germany, Austria, Serbia, Poland
- **UK**
- **North America**: United States, Canada, Mexico
- **GCC / Middle East**: Oman, UAE, Saudi Arabia

### Recent Expansion
- 2022: Acquired Skelia (Poland/Ukraine), launched Pwrteams, entered Canada

- **Aerospace and Defense** — AI-based aircraft detection for Estonian Air Force, cyber defense

- **Financial Services and Insurance**
- **Retail**
- **Telecommunications and Media**

## 8. Competitive Positioning

**Differentiators:**
- E-Government pedigree (Estonia's digital infrastructure)
- Employee-owned structure
- GCC government expertise (decade+ in Oman, UAE, Saudi Arabia)
- Microsoft ecosystem depth
- Nearshore delivery model (Latin America via Nearsure)

**Competitive landscape:**
- Mid-sized vs. Accenture, Capgemini, CGI, Deloitte
- In Nordics/Baltics: competes with TietoEVRY, CGI, regional players
- Strongest moat: government digital transformation + industrial/energy data platforms on Microsoft stack
- vs. Thoughtworks/EPAM: differentiates by combining strategy consulting with delivery + deep domain expertise
